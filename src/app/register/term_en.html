The Regulation and rules of the terres Catalunya Festival are available in catalan, spanish and english.

terres Catalunya, ECO & INTERNATIONAL FILM FESTIVAL is anually celebrated, and it takes place in the defined dates during the month of june.

This Festival is organized by FILMSNOMADES (Filmsnomades GS, S.L.) with the official support of the Agència Catalana de Turisme (ACT), Diputació de Tarragona and Ajuntament de Tortosa. The Festival has been born with the collaboration of APTUR, Associaçao Portuguesa de Turismologia, which is in charge of the organization of ART & TUR INTERNATIONAL FILM FESTIVAL.

1. FESTIVAL REGISTRATION, COMPETITIONS AND SECTIONS

terres Catalunya has 3 competitions in contest: TOURISM COMMUNICATION, DOCUMENTARIES and CORPORATE COMMUNICATION.

  • TOURISM COMMUNICATION. This competition contains advertisments (up to 1’ of duration) and promotional movies (up to 15’ of duration). Each film can be adhered to one of the categories below:
    • Touristic Destinations
    • Cultural Landscapes
    • Sports and Outdoor Activities
    • Routes, Expeditions and Travels
    • Hotels, Resorts and Spas
    • Events
    • Health and Wellness Tourism
    • Rural Tourism
    • Nature Tourism
    • Gastronomic experiences and Enotourism
    • Promotion of locations for film shooting
    • Animation
  • DOCUMENTARIES. This competition contains 3 categories:
    • Short Films
    • Feature Films
    • Webdocs
  • CORPORATE COMMUNICATION. This is a single category, although there can coexist productions of different formats in it.

In all cases, to formalize the registration, each user must:

  • Fill in the registration form individually for each film.
  • Pay the registration fee for each film.

Once started the registration process, each user will receive an email with the personal access keys to the terres Catalunya intranet. All the registration process of the films in contest will take place through the intranet. The users can raise any question during all the registration process to contact@terres.info.

2. PRODUCTION YEAR

All the films and campaigns registered in the Festival must have been produced after 1st January 2015.

3. ORIGINAL VERSIONS AND LANGUAGES

The Festival accepts inscriptions in any language. Although, the productions which are not originally made in catalan, spanish or english must have:

  • Subtitles in english or voice-off in english, spanish or catalan.
  • Synopsis written in english, spanish or catalan.

8. WINNERS NOTIFICATION

The enrolled films will be previously evaluated and winners will be notified in due time. Presence of winners participating in the Awards Ceremony in a location yet to be determined of Terres de l’Ebre, in Spain, is a moment of recognition, so it is strongly recommended to attend the Awards Ceremony in order to receive the prize.
